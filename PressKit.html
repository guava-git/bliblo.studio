About the Game

Free to Play

The market today is filled with games that are getting more and more complex, engaging, and… addictive. We love our games. They make us feel amazed, push us to better ourselves, and offer us an escape from our overwhelming lives.

Unfortunately, it's hard to deny that they can sometimes get in the way of our ability to focus and stay engaged during important tasks like school and work. A modern trend in game design to maximize dopamine for consistent, repetitive use is also being shown to reduce our attention spans and overall ability to motivate ourselves outside of screens.

Empower Your Intent (EYI) is a free-to-play atmospheric rhythm game that seeks to offer gamers an alternative. For those seeking to improve their attention spans and struggle with keeping away from their screens, Empower Your Intent offers a revolutionary approach to gaming.

Key Features

🎯 Mindful Design

Enforces planning and time management to build better digital consumption habits

🧘 Mindfulness Focus

Implements design that stimulates mindfulness and protects against habitual use

🚫 No Dopamine Traps

Actively avoids dopamine traps and external motivators

🎵 Music Therapy

Applies principles of music therapy and cognitive training to enhance mental wellbeing

Can games be used as a tool for self-development and a stronger brain?

BLIBLO STUDIO is answering that question with a loud, resounding "YES".

Community & Music

EYI is a beautiful game that can deeply calm you or give you an energy boost whenever you need it. The developer actively encourages community feedback and is offering to promote aspiring music producers by turning their tracks into levels for the game.

The developer also offers a royalty-free soundtrack that anyone can use in any personal and professional projects without worrying about licensing and royalties.
