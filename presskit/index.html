About the Game

Free to Play

The market today is filled with games that are becoming more complex, engaging, and… addictive. We love our games. They amaze us, push us to better ourselves, and offer us an escape from our overwhelming lives.

Unfortunately, it's hard to deny that they can sometimes get in the way of our ability to focus and stay engaged during important tasks, such as school and work. A modern trend in game design to maximize dopamine for consistent, repetitive use is being shown to reduce our attention spans and degrade our capacity to motivate ourselves to do difficult, enriching activities outside of screens.

Empower Your Intent (EYI) is a free-to-play atmospheric rhythm game that can deeply calm you or give you an energy boost whenever you need it. For those seeking to improve their attention spans and struggle with keeping away from their screens, Empower Your Intent offers an enriching gateway to your goals.

Can games be used as a tool for self-development and a stronger brain?

BLIBLO STUDIO answers that question with a loud, resounding "YES".

Key Features

Enhanced Mental State

Applies principles of music therapy and cognitive training to enhance mental wellbeing

No Instant Gratification

Actively avoids dopamine traps and external motivators/ rewards

Plan Before Playing

Enforces planning and time management to build better digital consumption habits

Build Mindfulness

Implements design that stimulates mindfulness and protects against habitual use

Community & Music

The developer actively encourages community feedback and is offering to promote aspiring music producers by turning their tracks into levels for the game.

The developer also offers a royalty-free soundtrack that anyone can use in any personal and professional projects without worrying about licensing and royalties.
